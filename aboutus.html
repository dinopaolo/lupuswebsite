- Our Mission -

To empower people by providing information about lupus and offering support, so their voices are heard and their condition diagnosed and managed effectively.

We want the improve the lives of people who have lupus

We want lupus patients to be entitled to specialised treatment

We want to provide lupus patients the best possible information about their condition

We want to give awareness to the public about lupus and its effects

We want to give lupus patients a voice and bring people together so they dont feel alone

We want to find a cure for lupus
